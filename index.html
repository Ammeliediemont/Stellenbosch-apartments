Upmarket, self-catering accommodation

These apartments are in a small block of flats, called Meulstroom, which is surrounded by historical houses. It is centrally situated, yet in a quiet area, five minutes walking distance from shops, restaurants, museums, art galleries the university and the Botanical Gardens.

The Eerste River, with picturesque walks along its banks, is only a stone’s throw away. Across the river is the university sports complex, Coetzenburg, which offers a variety of facilities, including a swimming pool and gym which maybe be used for a modest fee.
